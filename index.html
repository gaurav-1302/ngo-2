Chandausi Pravasi Samaj

Chandausi Pravasi Samaj is a community-focused initiative dedicated to fostering connections, organizing networking events, and promoting community building among Chandausi expatriates.

Know more!

Amodini

Amodini is a philanthropic endeavor committed to empowering underprivileged girls through weekly athletics camps, scholarships, free tuition, English and computer training, as well as hosting talent competitions and chess board donations.

Know more!

I Love Chandausi

I Love Chandausi is a community-driven movement focused on the betterment of Chandausi through social media campaigns, a Jan Seva Kendra offering government welfare scheme assistance, and the celebration of Chandausi Day.

Know more!

Welcome to Chandausi Samaj Where Hearts Unite for Change!

In the heart of Chandausi, our mission is to foster a community of compassion, dedication, and impact. We're not just an organization; we are a collective of kindred spirits dedicated to making a positive difference in the lives of our fellow residents. Every helping hand, no matter how big or small, has the power to create transformative change. Our vision is to rise together, empowering Chandausi to reach new heights of growth and prosperity.
